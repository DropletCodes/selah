About

Hello! My name is Caroline, and I'm a teenage girl with a passion for Jesus! I enjoy drawing, biking, and playing guitar.

This website serves as an encouragement and a reminder. I hope it reminds you of the wonder of God's creation, and that even in the valley, God is still with you (Psalm 23:4, Joshua 1:9) and still in control (Lamentations 3:37, Proverbs 19:21).

If you feel stressed or distant from God, this website is for you. There's no sign in, no paywall, no cookies. Just simple reminders that, as Rich Mullins said, our God is an awesome God!
