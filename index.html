Welcome!

How do I use this website?

I recommend going to the Slideshow tab first. Look at the picture on the screen. Just pause and marvel at God's creation. Then click the image and a new one will appear. There are one hundred images that appear in a random order.

What are the other tabs for?

The Quotes tab is full of encouraging Bible verses and Christian quotes. Click the Verse tab to see a random Bible verse. God can work through randomness, whether it's the song that plays when you put your playlist on shuffle or the verse that pops up on this tab. If you need prayer or have a story you want to share, go to Prayers.

Why did you make this website?

Life is busy, and distractions are everywhere. With everything going on, it can be hard to remember that God is still with you. This website is here to remind you.

Why is it called "Selah"?

"Selah" (Hebrew סֶלָה) is a term predominantly used in Psalms. While the precise meaning is unknown, it is thought to denote a rythmic or meditative pause, or a musical interlude. See this website for more information. The purpose of this website is to pause and reflect on God's power, creativity, and love. Let this be an interlude: in the middle of your busy life, stop and remember God.
